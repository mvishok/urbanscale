URBANSCALE

Our project focuses on simplifying the tedious process of real estate hunting. With increasing rates of false marketing, inaccurate accounts of events, and tampering with logistics, we aspire to follow the route of hard truths and statistics.Urbanscale accurately identifies the given location, providing a smooth and easy user experience. Enter the latitude and longitude of the place you want to inquire about. It goes through an intricate process of comparing the weighted sectors, summing them, to give an end score translated into a percentage.
